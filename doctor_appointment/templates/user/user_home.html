24 X 7 Services

ICU

Our ICU services are at the core of many of our life-saving treatments. Patients are monitored round the clock in sterile, infection-safe environments.

Ambulance

Our critical care ambulances provide timely and quality pre-hospital emergency care and interhospital transfer for serious and injured patients.

Radiological Investigations

Experience unparalleled convenience with our 24/7 radiological investigations. We prioritize accurate and prompt results anytime, day or night.

Pharmacy

Available round the clock, our pharmacy is well-stocked with high-quality medicines and manned by competent staff.

Laboratory

We have a 24/7 lab equipped with modern equipment utilizing the latest technology to help doctors and specialists administer evidence-based treatments.

Operation Theatre Services

Our OT complex is designed to work round the clock with state-of-the-art facilities to perform basic and advanced laparoscopic surgeries safely.

Emergency and Trauma Care

Our 24-hour emergency and trauma care protocols guarantee the highest levels of skill, expertise, and infrastructure for urgent cases.

Haemodialysis

The Dialysis Unit provides lifesaving treatment for patients with chronic nephrotic diseases, ensuring their ongoing health needs are met.
