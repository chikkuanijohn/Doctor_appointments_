About Us

ASDM Fam Hospital

In the short span of its existence, ASDM Fam Hospital has created its niche on the map of quality healthcare delivery in South India by touching nearly 2 million lives. Having earned the trust and loyalty of patients through the compassion of its caregivers and high-precision medical technology, the institution has emerged as the leading quaternary care facility in the region.

ASDM Fam Hospital, established in 2014, was a thoughtful initiative from the renowned ASDM (CMI) group of institutions to offer quality and affordable medical care to the common man. The legacy of ASDM in pioneering a distinctive culture in the educational sector has helped them carve an exemplary medical institution in Tamilnadu. The hospital is well laid out in a 40-acre green landscape in the serene setting of Tamilnadu and is the closest facility to the Thanjavur International Airport.

The consistent pursuit of achieving uncompromised excellence in healthcare has won ASDM Fam Hospital many accolades and appreciation on national and international platforms. The Hospital is accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI) with the gold seal for complying with the highest international standards on patient safety and quality of care. The hospital is also accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH), National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) and has received ISO 22000:2018 certification for its Food & Beverages services.

Our Vision & Mission

"To give life abundantly"

  • To provide the most advanced & scientific, curative, preventive & promotive healthcare that leads to an abundance of life.
  • To undertake specialized and holistic healthcare services of world standard and provide them to all sections of society, with special concern for the marginalized.
  • To grow into a model & global leader in healthcare, innovative teaching in medical sciences, & pioneering research on health.
